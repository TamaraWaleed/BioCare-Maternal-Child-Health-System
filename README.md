# BioCare Maternal & Child Health System

## Project Overview

During pregnancy, the mother needs health care and follow-up of her condition and the condition of the fetus, by conducting periodic examination, and continuing after childbirth and following up the child’s condition after the age of two. 

All information is recorded in the brochure and the information may be damaged or lost, so the idea of the project came to make an electronic copy and register all child and mother data and retrieve it when needed.
